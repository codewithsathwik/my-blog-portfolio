05 Feb 2026

Not sharing this post with anyone, this might be my last post in this site. Going to remove the site soon.

From talking to everyone to not even thinking about it—being no one's priority, no interaction, liked by none, drowned in loneliness. It’s been very hard to be like this. Being like this has cost me many opportunities in my life. Mentioning my mother for the first time: how she has sacrificed everything for me and my brother.

Apart from this, the last few months are my most precious months, because of my hard work. Being good at studies, good at my interested things. So many people have tried to harm me, but it helped me to work more.

Some people might think that I am egoistic, but let me tell you—it's not ego, it's survival. I've learned to protect my energy, to focus on what builds me up. Those "harms" you threw my way? They were fuel. They pushed me to work harder, day after day, building strength I didn't know I had.

Now, if I want to express myself, I can’t. The past incidents stop me from saying something real or trusting someone deeply. Every word feels like a risk, every hand extended a potential trap. I've been burned too many times, left alone. And I've realized one thing: no one is coming to save you in your hardest times.

At the end of the day, it's a son fulfilling the dreams of his mother, who sacrificed her entire life for me.

And I want to mention one thing: some people may win with their "politics," but when it comes to my own "ability," I definitely win.

Trying to do something big in the coming days—you don’t even expect it.
